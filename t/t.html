    Productgegevens voor adidas NMD R1 - Heren Schoenen

    Kleur: Base Green-Core Black-White

    With a unique combination of shapes, angles and materials, the NMD_R1 shoes pay homage to adidas’ innovation. Modern construction and bold archival details combine to form a dynamic trainer. With a two tone effect mesh upper and responsive boost™ underfoot, these shoes boast new levels of flexibility, stability and strength. A running inspired sneaker with some of adidas' top innovations.

    Features:

    • boost™'s energy returning properties keep every step charged with an endless supply of light, fast energy
    • Two tone effect mesh upper
    • Matte synthetic TPU tape on upper; Clear matte stretch TPU 3 Stripes
    • Branded heel pull
    • Moulded EVA midsole plugs for signature NMD aesthetic
    • Webbed rubber outsole.
